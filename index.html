Who am I?

3D freelancer, expert in modeling, texturing, and animation

I'm a skilled 3D freelancer with over two years of experience in low-poly modeling, sculpting, texturing, animating, and rendering, delivering high-quality results for architectural visualization, interior design, character creation, game assets, 3D prints, and logo animations.

What I do

My work can be divided into three areas:

Lowpoly

Lowpoly is used for game assets and 3D websites. It looks blocky but is lightweight for rendering and moving within games and websites. Adding textures helps maintain detail despite the lowpoly style

Highpoly

It is used for rendered projects purpose like home interiors, architecture, and 3D printing purpose. I usually stick to quad poly unless detailed sculpting is needed.

Animation

Motion graphics, typography, non-realistic character rigging, cartoons, logos, and more. I always strive to apply animation principles for more engaging results.
